Elxis 2008.1 patch

Elxis 2008.1 Nemesis rev2218 is an updated version of Elxis 2008.1 that fixes some issues on the Elxis 2008.1 distribution. It is important to notice that the Elxis major version did not changed. Only the revision number was changed from number 2204 to 2218. A patch to update Elxis 2008.1 to revision 2018 is also available. We strongly recommend you to immediately update to Elxis 2008.1 Nemesis rev2218!
